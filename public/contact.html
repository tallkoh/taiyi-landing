Contact

We read every reply.

Taiyi is a small Singapore-based project. There is no support queue, no ticketing system, no chatbot. We just read email.

For most things

taiyi.contact@gmail.com

Questions about your reading, the texts we cite, how the chart was calculated, billing, anything else. Replies usually go out Sunday evening after the letter ships.

To stop receiving letters

Click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any weekly letter — one click, no form. Or write to taiyi.contact@gmail.com with the subject "Unsubscribe".

To delete your data (PDPA)

Visit /delete. You'll receive a confirmation link by email; clicking it nulls your date, time, and place of birth immediately and queues the rest of the row for hard-deletion within 7 days.

For practitioners and press

Reply to the weekly letter or write to taiyi.contact@gmail.com with your enquiry. We're working slowly and don't take many calls.
